About Me

Background

I don't have much experience with coding, apart from casual interest. I started becoming interested in coding after a friend of mine graduated Epicodus and started her career in programming. I thought i'd give it a try to see if I liked it, and if I did, that I would go to for it! Aside from that I have listed my work experience here in order, from first job to latest job:

  1. Winter Park Veterinary Hospital: (2006-2009) I worked as a CVT in the hospital, and as a Laboratory Technician.
  2. Lush: Fresh Handmade Cosmetics: (2009-2016) I started as a Sales Associate before moving up to Inventory Specialist, Inventory Manager, Trainer, and then Junior Manager.
  3. Apple Inc.: (2016-Present) I started as a Sales Specialist and have moved on to Operations Specialist since then.

I am enjoying my time here at Epicodus so far, and I look forward to career in programming of my own someday soon!
